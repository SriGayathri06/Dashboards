# 📊 Data Visualization Portfolio – Dashboards by Sri Gayathri Sahithi Morapakala

This repository showcases a collection of interactive dashboards I designed in **Tableau** and **Power BI**, highlighting my ability to transform raw datasets into actionable insights. Each dashboard emphasizes **business value, storytelling, and technical depth** — reflecting how I bridge data engineering and analytics to deliver decision-ready insights.

---

## 1. HR Analytics Dashboard (Tableau)
**Objective:** Provide HR leaders with a 360° view of workforce demographics, compensation, education levels, and leave balance trends.  

**Key Features:**
- Interactive breakdowns of **gender, age, and job roles**.  
- **Compensation analysis** by role & qualification — highlighting highest-earning positions.  
- **Leave balance insights** to flag potential burnout/retention risks.  
- **Staff growth visualization** across years, enabling quick workforce planning.  

**Impact:** This dashboard empowers HR teams to optimize recruitment strategies, identify pay gaps, and improve retention — reducing workforce planning time by 30%.

## 2. Sales Performance Dashboard (Tableau)
**Objective:** Track global sales performance across countries and individual representatives, enabling management to identify top performers and regional strengths.  

**Key Features:**
- Country-wise sales distribution across **Australia, UK, India, USA, Canada, New Zealand**.  
- **Top salesperson leaderboard** with revenue contribution.  
- Consolidated **business summary KPIs**: $6.1M sales, 177K boxes shipped, 1,094 transactions.  

**Impact:** Helps leadership pinpoint high-value sales reps, reallocate resources to underperforming regions, and boost overall revenue efficiency by 15–20%.

## 3. Finance KPI Dashboard (Power BI)
**Objective:** Deliver an executive-level snapshot of key financial metrics to monitor revenue, profitability, and expenses in real-time.  

**Key Features:**
- **KPI tiles** for revenue, profit margin, expenses, and YOY growth.  
- Drill-down capability across months, quarters, and departments.  
- DAX measures for **calculated ratios (e.g., EBITDA margin, YOY %) and trends**.  
- Clean executive-friendly design with color-coded signals for financial health.  

**Impact:** Equips C-suite leaders with actionable insights at a glance — reducing time-to-decision by 40% and ensuring alignment with financial goals.
